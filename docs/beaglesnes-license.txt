  BeagleSNES - Super Nintendo Entertainment System (TM) emulator for the
  BeagleBoard-xM and BeagleBone Black platforms.

  (c) Copyright 2013         Andrew Henderson (hendersa@icculus.org)

  BeagleSNES is a GUI front-end that is integrated into to the Snes9x
  emulator, along with some hacks that shut off unneeded functionality
  and tune the performance for execution on the BeagleBoard-xM and
  BeagleBone Black platforms.
